About the Temple

The sacred Sri Istakameswara Swamy Temple at Gurazala, Palnadu District, Andhra Pradesh, was constructed in the 12th century by Nayakuralu Nagamma.

Devotees believe the temple is blessed by Naga Devata performing pradakshina around the shrine at night. Ancient lore says there is a hidden passage connecting this temple to Macherla Chennakesava Swamy Temple and Karampudi Temple.

This ancient temple, located at Burel Ground, radiates spiritual energy and attracts devotees from far and wide.
